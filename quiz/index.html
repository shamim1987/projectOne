How to Play:

This is a TWO player game.
Each player is given 10 multiple choice Questions selected at random.
Each question is worth 10 points each.
If there are 2 correct answers and you answer one correctly, you score 5 points.
A wrong response cuts 5 points.
The player with the most points at the end of 10 questions wins.
Enjoy!!
